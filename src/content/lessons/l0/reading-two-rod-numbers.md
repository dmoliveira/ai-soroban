Two-rod reading is where place value stops being abstract and starts becoming visible.

## Core idea

Read the rods from left to right by place:

- tens first
- ones second

In **42**, the left rod means **40** and the right rod means **2**.

## Common mistakes

- Reading each rod separately but forgetting to combine them.
- Ignoring an empty rod and losing the place meaning.

## Practice habit

Say the value in two steps first, then as one whole number:

- "four tens"
- "two ones"
- "forty-two"
