Complements become easier when they are felt as a pattern ladder instead of many separate facts.

## Core idea

For complements to five:

- 1 ↔ 4
- 2 ↔ 3

For complements to ten:

- 2 ↔ 8
- 3 ↔ 7
- 4 ↔ 6
- 5 ↔ 5

## Practice habit

Say both sides of the pair aloud until the ladder feels rhythmic.

## Common mistakes

- Remembering the pair only in one direction.
- Treating complements as memorization alone instead of movement helpers.
